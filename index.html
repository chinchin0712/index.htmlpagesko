Adulthood

a softer reset, a braver version of me

The Other Side of Me

Turning 18 made me realize there’s another side of me I’m still learning. I’m not just the jolly, clingy, or moody version people already know — I’m someone who wants to grow, unlearn, and become better in ways that actually matter. I’m starting to understand myself more deeply, even the parts I used to ignore.

Learning Something New

This chapter feels like a season of learning. Learning how to choose myself, how to be patient with my emotions, and how to face unfamiliar things without fear. I want to try new experiences, explore new skills, and allow myself to be a beginner without feeling ashamed.

Walking With God

As I step into adulthood, I also want to grow spiritually. I’m learning to follow God’s word, not perfectly, but sincerely. I want to trust His timing, seek His guidance, and let my faith shape the way I love, choose, and live. This is me choosing growth — with grace, faith, and intention.

What I Hope For

I hope to experience more of life — the quiet lessons, the unexpected joys, and even the hard moments that teach me strength. I want to keep learning, keep believing, and keep becoming someone I’ll be proud of, one step at a time.
